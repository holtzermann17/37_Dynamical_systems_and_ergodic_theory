Γ-equivariant


Let Γ be a compact Lie group acting linearly on V and let g be a mapping defined as g:V→V. Then g is Γ-equivariant if

g⁢(γ⁢v)=γ⁢g⁢(v)

for all γ∈Γ, and all v∈V.
Therefore if g commutes with Γ then g is Γ-equivariant.
