shadowing lemma


Let M be a Riemannian manifold, f:M→M a diffeomorphism and Λ⊂M a compact hyperbolic set for f. Then there is a neighborhood U of Λ such that for every δ>0 there is an ϵ>0 so that every ϵ-orbit (http://planetmath.org/PseudoOrbit) in U is δ-shadowed by an orbit of f.

Moreover, there is δ0>0 such that, if δ<δ0 and if the pseudo-orbit is bi-infinite, then the shadowing orbit is unique; and if Λ has a local product structure then the shadowing orbit is in Λ.
