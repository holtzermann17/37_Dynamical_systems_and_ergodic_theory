pseudo-orbit


Let (X,d) be a metric space, f:X→X a function, and let ϵ>0. An ϵ-pseudo-orbit for f is a sequence {xn:n∈ℤ,a<n<b}, where -∞≤a<b≤∞, such that d⁢(xn+1,f⁢(xn))<ϵ for all a<n<b. A periodic pseudo-orbit is an infinite pseudo-orbit {xn} such that there is some p with xn+p=xn for all n.

Given δ>0, the pseudo-orbit {xn:a<n<b} is said to be δ-shadowed by the orbit of x, if d⁢(xn,fn⁢(x))<δ for all a<n<b.
