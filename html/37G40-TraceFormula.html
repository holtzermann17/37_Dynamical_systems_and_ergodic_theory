trace formula


Let Γ be a compact Lie group acting on V and let Σ⊂Γ be a Lie subgroup. Then

dim⁡Fix⁡(Σ)=∫σ∈Σtrace⁡(σ)

where ∫ denotes the normalized Haar integral on Σ and Fix⁡(Σ) is the fixed-point subspace of Σ .

References

  • GSS Golubitsky, Martin. Stewart, Ian. Schaeffer, G. David: Singularities and Groups in Bifurcation Theory (Volume II). Springer-Verlag, New York, 1988.
