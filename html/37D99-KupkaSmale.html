Kupka-Smale


A diffeomorphism f mapping a smooth manifold M to itself is called a Kupka-Smale diffeomorphism if

  1. 1.

    every periodic point of f is hyperbolic;

  2. 2.

    for each pair of periodic points p,q of f, the intersection between the stable manifold of p and the unstable manifold of q is transversal.
