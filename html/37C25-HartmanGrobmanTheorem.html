Hartman-Grobman theorem


Let U and V be open subsets of a Banach space E such that 0∈U∩V. If a diffeomorphism f:U→V has 0 as a hyperbolic fixed point, then f and D⁢f⁢(0) are locally topologically conjugate at 0, i.e. there are neighborhoods U~ and V~ of 0 and a homeomorphism h:U~→V~ such that D⁢f⁢(0)⁢h=h⁢f.
